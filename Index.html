ABOUT ME

Maite Gijbels

Student Digital Design

Hello! I am Maite Gijbels, a 17-year-old Student with a passion for design. I’m currently enrolled in PXL, a University in Hasselt, Belgium, and study Digital Design.

One of my favorite things to do in my spare time is listening to music. During this I enjoy playing with different designtools, and learn new things. Once in a while I like to entertain myself by watching movies, cooking, baking or creating new recipies.

Overall, I’m a calm, organized and independent person. Someone who loves to be creative, do a lot of things, but also likes to relax whenever she can.

PROJECTS

In this part of my portfolio you can find some of the projects I worked on. These projects were made in my classes, and also in my spare time to develop my skills even more. I will also tell a little bit about the different projects.

Design

In our Graphic Skills lessons we made a lot of designs in Photoshop and Illustrator. The most important design we did in Photoshop, is the first picture. This design reflects the different things we’ve learned during our lessons. This was a design we had to recreate for our PE (permanent evaluation).

The second design was a wireframe we had to recreate in our UI Design lesson. This was an exercise on using grids. It made me realise grids are very helpful when creating wireframes, because it makes everything look more organised.

Web

These are some exercises we did in our class ‘Web Essentials’. The first one is a chessboard we created with tables.

The second picture represents one of the exercises from when we just learned to work with CSS. We created a page with different festivals, learned to navigate through the page itself, but also to different pages.

Spare Time

In one of our first lessons UI Design, we had to sketch an app. I chose to sketch an app like spotify, with the same sort of features just different colors. Once I got home I wanted to try and make different wireframes based on my sketches, and that is what you can see in the first picture.

The second picture is the starting page of the app. These wireframes are still in shades of grey, because I am still working on it. Once I am completely done I will start choosing the colors I would like to use for this app.

This is a small project I worked on for my eighteenth birthday. It was a good excercise to practise my Figma and Photoshop skills.

This picture is the invitation I made for my upcoming birthday. With all the inspiration I gathered from different sites, I came up with this design.

I think it is something that really suits me as a person. The invitation is quite minimalistic, which makes it look calming and welcoming.
